Sumikko Gurashi

☆ LIFE IN A CORNER ☆

SHIROKUMA

Shirokuma is a shy bear who migrated south to

avoid the cold. Shirokuma feels most calm

while drinking hot tea in a corner.

PENGUIN?

Am I even a penguin?

Penguin has no confidence.

TONKATSU

The end of a tonkatsu.

Made of 1% meat, 99% fat.

Left behind for being too oily...

NEKO

Shy and timid, Neko usually hides in the corner.

Neko worries about its body shape.

TOKAGE

Is actually the last of the dinosaurs.

It pretends to be a lizard to avoid being caught.

TAPIOCA

They are the leftovers in the cup as only

milk tea was drunk up earlier.

FUROSHIKI

Shirokuma’s luggage.

Used to claim corners and when

Shirokuma is cold.

ZASSOU

A positive weed.

Admires florists and dreams of

made into a bouquet one day.

NISETSUMURI

Is actually just a slug with a shell.

I'm sorry for tricking you…

This is Hokori. A careless bunch that often piles up near corners.
